Whats it like to live in Coventry?

    Top reasons to be in Coventry

  1. Easy to get to big cities via train e.g. Coventry to Birmingham in less than 20 minutes & to London Euston in an hour
  2. Great variety of resteraunts/fast food
  3. Hosts big events like BBC Radio 1 Big Weekend with acts such as Calvin Harris, Ed Sheeran & KSI
  4. Decent nightlife
  5. Cheap

    Top reasons to avoid Coventry

  1. Weather / Colour palette (Always grey for 340/365 days)
