What is a Coventry?

Coventry... a city located in the West Midlands, home of BINLEY MEGA CHIPPY.

Coventry is 19 miles and is the most central city in England. With a population of 366,785, Coventry is known for the "Coventry Blitz" which was a series of bombing raids that took place during the Second World War due to having high concentration of armaments, munitions, aircraft and aero-engine plants which contributed greatly to WW2.
